Stud Legacy

Retired to Shadai Stallion Station, Kitasan Black quickly proved himself as a sire. His son Equinox won both the Autumn Tenno Sho and Arima Kinen ‚Äî mirroring his father's final campaign. Sol Oriens captured the 2023 Satsuki Sho and Croix du Nord the 2025 Japanese Derby ‚Äî both races Kitasan Black himself could never win, completing the legacy through his children.

In retirement, Kitasan Black was famously known for challenging horses in adjacent pastures to race along the fences ‚Äî staying so fit that his caretakers resorted to heavier horseshoes (which only made him stronger). They eventually used this to their advantage, placing overweight horses nearby so Kitasan would run them into shape.
