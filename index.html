About the Legend

Kitasan Black (キタサンブラック) is one of the most celebrated Japanese Thoroughbred racehorses in modern history. Owned by the famous enka singer Saburo Kitajima, he captured the hearts of racing fans with his incredible stamina, front-running style, and undeniable charisma.


Today, Kitasan Black's legacy lives on not only through his highly successful progeny (such as Equinox) but also as a beloved, energetic, and festival-loving character in the hit multimedia franchise Uma Musume: Pretty Derby.

Career Highlights

During his racing career, Kitasan Black dominated the Japanese turf, earning the title of Japanese Horse of the Year twice (2016, 2017) and being inducted into the Japan Racing Association Hall of Fame in 2020.
